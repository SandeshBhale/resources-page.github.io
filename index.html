Bipolar Disorder

Awareness & Knowledge

Symptoms

  • Extreme mood swings (mania and depression)
  • Increased energy during mania
  • Sadness during depression
  • Impaired judgment and risky behavior
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Changes in sleep and appetite
  • Feelings of hopelessness or guilt
  • Thoughts of suicide (severe cases)

Precautions

  • Medication compliance
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Therapy and counseling
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • Stress reduction techniques
  • Avoid substance abuse
  • Build a support system
  • Recognize early warning signs

Diagnosis

  • Consult a healthcare professional
  • Undergo medical tests and evaluations
  • Consider family history and symptoms
  • Seek a second opinion if necessary
  • Follow recommended treatment plans

Myths

  • People with mental illness are always dangerous
  • Mental health problems are a sign of weakness
  • You can just "snap out" of mental illness
  • Only "crazy" people go to therapy
  • Mental health issues only affect adults
